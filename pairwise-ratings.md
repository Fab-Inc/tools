A tool for pairwise comparisons of text according to a criterion. Upload a CSV file, then rate each pair by clicking or pressing 1 (left) or 2 (right) based on the displayed criteria. Expand the rating instructions to know how to rate/choose between texts. Pairs are grouped by the same criterion, if it switches to a new one you'll see a notification at the top. Navigate between pairs with arrow keys, and download your judgments anytime as a CSV with the chosen option appended.
